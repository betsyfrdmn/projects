## Projects Overview

### anchor_client_strategy

Focus: Client segmentation and strategic analysis using behavioral and transactional data.
Methods: Exploratory data analysis, clustering, time series visualization, and principal component analysis.
Languages: R
Libraries: readr, tidyverse, forecast, stats

---

### college_data_etl_and_analysis

Focus: End-to-end ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) and analysis of college-level educational data.
Methods: Data ingestion, database connectivity, cleaning, transformation, and statistical summaries.
Languages: Python, SQL
Libraries: pandas, numpy, sqlalchemy, psycopg2

---

### editorials_vs_reportages

Focus: Text analysis comparing editorials and reportages to identify linguistic and stylistic differences.
Methods: Natural language processing, tokenization, frequency analysis, and comparative text statistics.
Languages: R
Libraries: quanteda, quanteda.textstats, tidyverse, dplyr, patchwork

---

### hiv_clinical_trial

Focus: Statistical analysis of HIV clinical trial data to evaluate treatment outcomes.
Methods: Descriptive statistics, multiple regression, and partial F tests for model assessment.
Languages: R
Libraries: tidyverse, stats, car

---

### house_values_and_rail_trails

Focus: Examining the relationship between housing values and proximity to rail trails and infrastructure.
Methods: Exploratory data analysis, correlation analysis, and regression modeling.
Languages: R
Libraries: readr, tidyverse, GGally, viridis, corrplot, patchwork, ggpubr

---

### linguistic_campaigning

Focus: Analysis of political or advocacy-related language to understand messaging strategies.
Methods: Text mining, sentiment analysis, and thematic comparison across documents.
Languages: R
Libraries: quanteda, quanteda.textstats, quanteda.extras, tidyverse, ggraph, gt, stringr, dplyr

---

### religion_in_the_news

Focus: Media representation of religion and how religious topics are framed in news coverage.
Methods: Text analysis, topic modeling, frequency comparison, and narrative framing analysis.
Languages: Python, R
Libraries: pandas, re, tidyverse, quanteda, quanteda.textstats, quanteda.extras, ggraph, tidytext, textdata, gt, patchwork, udpipe
